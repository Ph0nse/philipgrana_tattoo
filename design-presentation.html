Design Decisions

01
Dark luxury aesthetic — ink-black with warm gold accents mirrors the permanence and prestige of fine tattoo work, immediately communicating the artist's positioning.
02
Three-font hierarchy — Cinzel for drama, Cormorant Garamond for editorial warmth, Space Mono for clinical precision. Together they balance art and professionalism.
03
Asymmetric gallery grid — varied column spans (5/4/3) create a dynamic editorial layout rather than a static portfolio grid, with lightbox and category filtering built in.
04
Bilingual EN / ES — full site translation toggle for English and Spanish audiences, with all strings handled client-side for instant switching.
05
Scroll-reveal & active nav — sections animate in as the user scrolls, and the navigation highlights the current section, giving the site a refined editorial feel throughout.
