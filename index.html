Healing & care

Aftercare instructions

Proper aftercare shapes how your tattoo heals and ages. Follow these steps closely for the first few weeks.

01

Leave the initial bandage on for 2–4 hours (or as instructed), then remove it gently.

02

Wash with lukewarm water and a fragrance-free antibacterial soap, patting dry with a clean paper towel — never rub.

03

Apply a thin layer of fragrance-free, tattoo-safe moisturizer 2–3 times a day. A little goes a long way.

04

Avoid soaking the tattoo — no swimming, baths, or saunas — for at least two weeks.

05

Keep it out of direct sunlight while healing, and use SPF once healed to protect the linework and colour long-term.

06

Wear loose, breathable clothing over the area and avoid tight fabric rubbing against it.

07

Don't pick or scratch any peeling or scabbing skin — let it flake off naturally.

08

Contact the studio if you notice excessive redness, swelling, or discharge — signs of possible infection.
